About us


Why carpooling ?

We own zippy cars. We rarely get to enjoy driving them in the city. We love long-drives on highways. We travel between cities. We dither from self-driving owing to cost considerations.

We have seats vacant. We can Offer Seats.We find it difficult to get confirmed tickets by train for an impromptu trip.

Ditto, even if we plan in advance, for the festive season. We find buses quoting rates through the roof. We are seeking an alternative. We Search for seats. Sign Up with Carpooling. The green enabler .

Offer. Search. Match. Unleash the latent power of your resources. Benefit mutually. All the way!



Philosophy

Team Carpooling comprises of value conscious professionals. We abhor waste. There are many dormant assets we own.

Idling them makes our hearts bleed. Carpooling aims at connecting the source and sink. Completing the circuit. Making the energy flow. Lighting lamps of happiness.

Carpooling embarks on this journey of value unleashing by enabling synergy of passengers heading in the same direction!

carpooling will be enhancing the value proposition by bringing in other components of the travel ecosystem in a phased manner.
